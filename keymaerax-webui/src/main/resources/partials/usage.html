Using KeYmaera X

The page "Your Models" lists the available models and allows you to upload new models. You can list the proofs of a model by clicking on "Show proofs", and start a new proof by selecting "Start new proof" from the action icons.

Click on the model name to look at the model. Click on the description to get additional information.

The page "Your Proofs" lists the available proofs and allows you to load and review proofs.

The proof view indicates your tasks in a proof agenda. Below the agenda, depending on your screen resolution, you can find the proof obligation of the selected agenda item. Hover the mouse over formulas in the proof obligation to see your options for proof search locally at that formula. Step ( Step) performs a single proof step based on the top-level operator in that formula. Hide ( Hide) performs weakening, which means it removes the formula from the proof obligation.

Instead of automated proof search, you can manually interact with the prover by clicking an operator in a formula (e.g., the conjunction symbol), which picks a simplifying step based on the top-level operator in the formula.

Right-clicking on a formula opens a menu showing options relevant to that formula, complete with their definitions. If a particular proof step accepts inputs, enter them by typing in the text box. To run a tactic, click on its name.

Not sure where to start? The menu on the top of the screen allows you to browse through commonly used proof steps. To apply a step, click on its name. This is also where you can run general-purpose proof search tactics like Auto and Normalize, which performs many of the same steps as Auto, but does not attempt expensive operations such as solving differential equations.
