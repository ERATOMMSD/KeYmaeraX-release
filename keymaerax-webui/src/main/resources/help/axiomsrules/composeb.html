The composeb axiom splits the safety of a sequential composition a;b, which runs a followed by b, into successive safety properties [a][b]P showing that after all runs of a it is the case that all runs of b are safe, so satisfy P.

See also

  • MR monotonicity tactic instead proves an intermediate condition that always holds after a and is strong enough to imply the safety of b. Using the monotonicity principle can better separate the proof but requires an intermediate condition transporting all required knowledge through a to b.
  • chase tactic equivalently chases formulas away by reducing them to structurally simpler formulas. Chase a formula way using Alt-Click or Option-Click.
  • unfold tactic automatically advances all such easy steps of a proof while stopping at loops and ODEs.
