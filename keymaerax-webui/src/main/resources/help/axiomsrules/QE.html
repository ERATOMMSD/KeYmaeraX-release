Quantifier elimination QE decides truth of formulas in real arithmetic.

QE may potentially take a long time. Reducing the number of variables that occur in formulas helps. Use abbrv to simplify formulas together with hideL and hideR to hide irrelevant assumptions and conclusions prior to QE. How to abbreviate:

  • Click Edit, enclose a term with abbrv(...) for anonymous abbreviation or abbrv(..., x) to introduce abbreviation x
  • Click proof hint Abbrv and enter an abbreviation name and a term that should be abbreviated

See also

  • Tools → Find counterexample to get potentially faster feedback whether the sequent might be true or false.
  • Tools → Find assumptions to get hints on how to strengthen the assumptions to make the sequent provable.
